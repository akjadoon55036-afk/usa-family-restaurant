Our Story

A True Family Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Reynolds, Indiana, USA Family Restaurant — also beloved as Mom's Country Kitchen — has been the cornerstone of this small community for years. When you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family.

We take pride in every plate. Our portions are generous, our prices are fair, and our recipes are rooted in honest, home-style American cooking. From fluffy biscuits and gravy at sunrise to fall-off-the-bone ribs at dinnertime, we cook from the heart.

Located just one block east of the IN-43 & US-421 intersection, we're a favorite stop for locals and travelers alike passing through White County.
